Operational Methodology运作方式：

  • Grant-making rather than operational foundation

定位于资助型为主，运作型为辅

  • Focus on project design, partner selection, monitor & evaluation, and research outputs

专注于项目设计、合作伙伴遴选、监督与评估、以及研究成果的产出

  • Characterizing in policy research and policy advocacy on poverty alleviation

导向于扶贫济困资助成效的政策启示与调查研究

  • Progressive implementation, focus on priorities and pilot projects

循序渐进，量力而行，突出重点

    • e.g. education and innovation projects

优先教育资助，搭建创新平台

  • Build mutual beneficial relationship with NGO partners and government

与政府管理部门与相关NGO组织建立良好合作关系

  • Targeted on 4-star standard requirements to set up and run this foundation

以AAAA标准构建基金会治理框架，力争两年内获得政府管理部门AAAA以上级别认证
